## CHECK-IN, BAGGAGE DROP & GATE CLOSURE

- **Web check-in window**: Opens 48 hours before scheduled departure on www.skyjet.example and SkyJet app. Closes 60 minutes before departure for domestic, 90 minutes for international. Free seat selection from T-48h for Business and SkyMiles Gold/Platinum; ₹250–₹1,500 charge for Economy seat selection depending on seat type (standard / extra-legroom / front-row).
- **Airport kiosk check-in**: Available at all SkyJet hubs and 22 partner airports. Closes 45 minutes before departure (domestic) and 75 minutes (international). Prints boarding pass and bag tags; passenger drops tagged bag at the dedicated Bag Drop counter.
- **Counter check-in (assisted)**: Opens 3 hours before departure (domestic) and 4 hours (international). Closes 45 minutes (domestic) and 75 minutes (international) before scheduled departure. Passengers arriving after counter closure are treated as no-show; rebooking subject to fare rules and availability.
- **Baggage drop deadline**: Same as counter check-in closure — 45 minutes domestic, 75 minutes international. Bags presented after this deadline cannot be loaded and must be re-booked on a later flight as cargo or excess baggage.
- **Boarding gate**: Opens 45 minutes before departure for domestic, 60 minutes for international. **Gate closes 20 minutes before departure**, after which boarding pass is invalidated regardless of prior check-in. Passengers must clear security and reach the gate at least 25 minutes before departure.
- **Required documents**: Domestic — government photo ID (Aadhaar, passport, driving licence, voter ID, PAN). International — passport with min 6 months validity, valid visa for destination, return/onward ticket if required.

## CABIN BAGGAGE & CHECKED BAGGAGE ALLOWANCE

- **Cabin (carry-on) — Economy [ECO]**: 1 piece, max 7 kg, max dimensions 55 × 35 × 25 cm. Plus 1 personal item (laptop bag or handbag) max 3 kg.
- **Cabin — Premium Economy [PEC]**: 1 piece, max 10 kg, same dimensions. Plus 1 personal item max 3 kg.
- **Cabin — Business [BUS] / First [FST]**: 2 pieces, max 8 kg each (total 16 kg), same dimensions. Plus 1 personal item max 3 kg.
- **Checked — Economy domestic [ECO][DOM]**: 15 kg free | **Premium Economy**: 25 kg | **Business**: 35 kg | **First**: 40 kg.
- **Checked — Economy international [ECO][INT]**: 23 kg (1 piece) for Asia / Middle East; 30 kg (1 piece) for Europe / Australia | **Premium Economy**: 2 × 23 kg | **Business**: 2 × 32 kg | **First**: 3 × 32 kg.
- **Excess baggage charges (domestic)**: ₹600 per extra kg in Economy and Premium Economy; ₹450/kg in Business and First.
- **Excess baggage charges (international)**: US$25/kg to Asia/Middle East; US$40/kg to Europe/Australia. Pre-purchased excess online is 20% cheaper than airport rate.
- **Prohibited in checked bags**: Power banks, spare lithium batteries, e-cigarettes/vapes, loose lithium cells, matches, lighters with liquid fuel. These must travel in cabin baggage only.
- **Prohibited in cabin**: Liquids > 100 ml per container (unless purchased airside), sharp objects > 6 cm blade, firearms, fireworks, flammable gases. Liquids must be in a transparent 1-litre resealable bag.
- **Special items**: Sports equipment (golf bag, surfboard, bicycle) — must be declared 48 hours in advance; flat fee ₹2,500 domestic / US$75 international per item. Musical instruments up to cabin dimensions ride free; larger instruments need a paid extra seat (CBBG — "cabin baggage seat").
- **Fragile / Valuables**: SkyJet does not accept liability for cash, jewellery, electronics, or fragile items placed in checked baggage. Carry them in cabin.

## BOARDING PROCESS & PRIORITY ZONES

- **Boarding starts**: 45 minutes before departure (domestic), 60 minutes (international). Boarding closes 20 minutes before departure — gate closure is firm.
- **Boarding zones — in order**:
  - **Zone 1 [PRM]**: Business, First, SkyMiles Platinum, passengers requiring assistance (wheelchair, unaccompanied minors, families with infants).
  - **Zone 2 [PRM]**: Premium Economy, SkyMiles Gold, codeshare partner elites.
  - **Zone 3**: Economy rows 30 and behind (rear of cabin) — back-to-front boarding.
  - **Zone 4**: Economy rows 16–29 (middle cabin).
  - **Zone 5**: Economy rows 1–15 (front cabin), last to board.
- **Gate change policy**: Any gate change is announced over the airport PA, on the SkyJet app push notification, and on departure information screens. Passengers are responsible for reaching the new gate before the 20-minute cutoff.
- **Last call / no-show**: Three boarding calls are made — initial call, final call (10 minutes before departure), and last call (5 minutes before departure). After last call, the gate is closed and the passenger is marked no-show. Checked baggage is offloaded for security reasons, causing a delay typically of 10–15 minutes.
- **Stand-by upgrades**: At-gate paid upgrades to Premium Economy or Business are offered 90 minutes before departure if seats are available. Prices start at ₹3,500 (domestic short-haul) and US$199 (international short-haul).

## SPECIAL ASSISTANCE — WHEELCHAIR, MINORS, PETS, MEDICAL

- **Wheelchair assistance [WCHR]**: Request at booking, in "Manage Booking", or via call centre at least 48 hours before departure. Free service. Three categories: WCHR (can walk up steps but needs chair on tarmac), WCHS (cannot walk stairs), WCHC (immobile, requires assistance throughout). Dedicated wheelchair counter is to the left of each SkyJet check-in row.
- **Unaccompanied Minor (UM)**: Available for children aged 5–11 travelling alone. Fee ₹2,500 domestic / US$80 international per direction. Must be booked through call centre. UM is escorted by SkyJet staff from check-in to handover at destination — escort details must be provided in writing. Not available on flights with technical stops.
- **Infants (under 2)**: Travel on adult lap free domestically; 10% of adult fare on international. Bassinet seats (limited per flight) must be requested at booking. One adult may carry only one infant. A second infant requires its own seat at child fare.
- **Pets in cabin**: Small dogs and cats only (combined pet + carrier weight ≤ 7 kg, carrier max 40 × 30 × 20 cm). Limited to 2 cabin pets per flight, first-come first-served. Fee ₹4,000 domestic / US$150 international. Service animals travel free with valid documentation. Not allowed on Business / First class cabins for hygiene reasons.
- **Pets in hold**: Cats, dogs, rabbits in IATA-approved kennel. Fee ₹6,000 domestic / US$300 international. Not accepted on flights longer than 8 hours or to destinations with quarantine restrictions.
- **Medical clearance (MEDA)**: Required if passenger has had surgery in last 10 days, uses an on-board oxygen concentrator (POC), is in 32nd–36th week of pregnancy (single) or 28th–32nd week (twins). Submit medical form ≥ 72 hours before departure. Travel not permitted after 36 weeks (single) or 32 weeks (twins) of pregnancy.

## SAFETY, RESTRICTED ITEMS & DANGEROUS GOODS

- **Liquids in cabin**: Each container ≤ 100 ml; all containers must fit in one transparent resealable bag ≤ 1 litre. One bag per passenger. Medicines, baby food, and duty-free purchases (sealed STEB bag) are exempt.
- **Power banks**: Allowed only in cabin baggage. Capacity ≤ 100 Wh — no approval needed; 100–160 Wh — airline approval required, max 2 per passenger; > 160 Wh — not permitted at all.
- **Spare lithium batteries**: Cabin only; each cell must be individually protected (original packaging or terminals taped). Devices with installed batteries (laptop, camera, drone) may travel in checked baggage but must be powered off completely (not in sleep mode).
- **E-cigarettes and vape devices**: Cabin only. Charging on board is prohibited.
- **Firearms**: Only sporting arms accepted, declared and pre-approved 72 hours before flight, ammunition limited to 5 kg per passenger in original packaging. Not accepted to several international destinations — check before booking.
- **Sharp items**: Knives, scissors > 6 cm blade, box cutters not allowed in cabin. Small grooming scissors (≤ 6 cm) and disposable razors permitted.
- **Sporting and outdoor**: Compressed gas cylinders (camping stoves, scuba tanks) not accepted unless completely empty and inspected. Bicycle CO₂ cartridges max 2 per passenger.
- **Medical items**: Liquid medication > 100 ml allowed in cabin with prescription. Syringes for personal use allowed with documentation. Portable Oxygen Concentrators (POC) require MEDA clearance (see Special Assistance).

## LOST & FOUND, COMPLAINTS, CONTACT

- **Lost on board or at gate**: Report at SkyJet baggage service desk in the arrival hall within 24 hours. Items are logged into the central system; tracking link sent by email/SMS. Items held for 30 days at origin, then transferred to central lost property (Bengaluru HQ) and held for 90 more days.
- **Delayed / damaged / lost checked baggage**: Report at the baggage service desk in the arrival hall before leaving the airport — a Property Irregularity Report (PIR) reference is mandatory for any claim. SkyJet aims to deliver delayed bags within 48 hours; reimbursement up to ₹5,000 (domestic) / US$200 (international) for verified emergency purchases against receipts. Bags declared lost after 21 days; compensation per Montreal Convention or DGCA CAR Section 3 Series M Part IV.
- **Flight delay / cancellation compensation (per DGCA CAR)**:
  - **Domestic delay 2–4 hours**: Refreshments and meal vouchers.
  - **Delay > 4 hours or overnight**: Hotel accommodation, meals, and airport transfers if delay overnight, or re-accommodation on the next available SkyJet flight at no charge.
  - **Cancellation < 24 hours notice or denied boarding due to overbooking**: Full refund OR alternative flight, plus compensation as per DGCA scale (₹5,000–₹10,000 domestic; up to US$400 international).
- **Complaints and feedback**: Submit through SkyJet app "Help" tab, www.skyjet.example/feedback, or email care@skyjet.example. SkyJet commits to acknowledge within 24 hours and resolve within 14 working days. Unresolved complaints can be escalated to DGCA AirSewa portal (airsewa.gov.in) with the SkyJet case reference number.
- **Customer service phone numbers (24×7)**: India toll-free 1800-123-7539 | International +91-80-6677-7539 | SkyMiles Platinum priority line +91-80-6677-7500 | Group desk +91-80-6677-7510 | Cargo +91-80-6677-7520.

## CODESHARE, INTERLINE & THROUGH-CHECK-IN

- **Codeshare partners (marketed by SkyJet, operated by partner)**: Singapore Airlines (SQ), Japan Airlines (JL), Qatar Airways (QR), Lufthansa (LH), Virgin Atlantic (VS), ANA (NH), Cathay Pacific (CX). Bookings show "SJ flight number operated by [partner]".
- **Interline partners (separate-ticket through-fare and baggage handling)**: All codeshare partners plus Emirates (EK), Etihad (EY), Air France (AF), KLM (KL), Turkish (TK), Thai Airways (TG). Through-baggage check-in is available only when both tickets are issued on a single booking record (PNR) or with confirmed interline e-ticket; SkyJet cannot through-check baggage on two unrelated tickets.
- **Through-check-in cutoff**: Bag tagged to final destination only if connection at intermediate airport is ≥ 90 minutes (domestic-to-international) or ≥ 60 minutes (international-to-international same alliance). Below that, passenger must collect, clear customs/immigration, and re-check at next airline counter.
- **Mile earning on partner flights**: 25–125% of base earning rate depending on partner and booking class. Status credit awarded on flights operated by SkyJet only, not on partner-operated flights — see SkyMiles terms.
- **Connecting flight protection**: When all segments are on a single SkyJet e-ticket (including partner codeshare segments), SkyJet protects the connection — if the first segment delay causes a missed connection, SkyJet rebooks on the next available flight at no charge, with hotel and meals if the wait is overnight.
